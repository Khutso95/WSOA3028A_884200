Design Reflection

Author: Khutso Nkadimeng

25 Apr 2020

Character growth

To grow my character, I have decided to create chaos around him at the most critical time of his company. I have done this by including the Press section, my research shows that a lot of people have a Featured section in their websites where they list different publications that featured them, typically, these are positive interviews or articles on their work. I will not follow this norm; the press section will be used to integrate the world around my character and this website. This arrangement will allow me to also explore his history, motivation and attitude.

Language

I use familiar and ordinary language throughout the entire website. This is in part because of my language constraints and more deliberately, to enable easy consumption of my content. I try being crafty in how I arrange the words for entertainment purposes, and I break a lot of material into smaller pieces to make them easy to read.

Grammar remains a major problem to me, to mitigate this I used Grammarly and even after that a few friends reviewing my work still found errors. So, I am working under the assumption that I still have a lot of errors to correct.

UX

The direction I took from the beginning has not changed, I intend to maintain a minimalistic and responsive website. I also want to have a progress bar for readers.

I battled with the rationale behind the back button, the reason being that most browsers and devices already provide such buttons. But a few reviews were enough to show that back buttons are widely used, and some browsers and iPhones may not have them, so I included them for redundancy. I must say, the site felt better after including them. Now they feel necessary.

Going forward I will be using Laws of UX and the Nielsen Norman Group as guides for my UX design.

Accessibility

I plan to do more research into how to make my site bot friendly, I am also using simple language to accommodate different readers. I intend to use MDN Speech API to give users an option to listen to every post.
